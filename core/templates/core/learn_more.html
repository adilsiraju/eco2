Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about sustainable investing with Ecovest.

How does Ecovest vet the projects?

We conduct thorough due diligence on all projects through a multi-step verification process. This includes financial analysis, impact assessment, site visits, background checks on project managers, and regulatory compliance verification. Only projects that pass our rigorous criteria are listed on the platform.

What returns can I expect on my investments?

Returns vary by project type and risk profile, typically ranging from 7-15% annually. Each project listing includes detailed projected returns information. Remember that all investments carry some risk, and actual returns may differ from projections.

How is my environmental impact calculated?

We use internationally recognized methodologies to calculate environmental impact metrics for each project. These include carbon emissions reduction (measured in kg of CO2e), energy saved (kWh), water conserved (liters), and waste diverted from landfills (kg). Impact is attributed proportionally based on your investment amount.

Is there a minimum investment amount?

Yes, the minimum investment varies by project, typically starting at ₹5,000. This low entry point is designed to make sustainable investing accessible to more people while ensuring meaningful participation in projects.

How liquid are my investments?

Project investments typically have fixed terms ranging from 1-5 years, depending on the project type. Some projects offer early exit options, but these may include exit fees. Details on liquidity are clearly provided in each project description.

Is Ecovest regulated?

Yes, Ecovest operates in full compliance with all relevant securities regulations and is registered with the appropriate financial authorities. We maintain rigorous standards for transparency, investor protection, and anti-money laundering compliance.
