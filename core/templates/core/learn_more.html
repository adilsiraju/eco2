Our Mission

Ecovest was founded in 2025 as a final-year BE project by Adil Siraju, with a vision to blend financial returns with environmental impact. We’re a team of passionate students empowering investors to fund sustainable initiatives and innovative startups.

Through transparency and cutting-edge tech, we connect you to opportunities in renewable energy, water conservation, recycling, and green technology—making every investment count.

AI-Powered Impact Prediction

Our AI model, built from scratch by us, predicts the environmental impact of your investments. Trained on real-world data from 50+ global sustainability projects, it calculates carbon reductions, energy savings, and water conservation with precision.

Analyzing investment amounts, categories, duration, scale, location, and technology, our AI offers insights to maximize your impact—proof of what BE students can achieve!

Invest in Startup Companies

Ecovest goes beyond initiatives—we’ve added the ability to invest in eco-friendly startups. Buy shares in emerging companies innovating in clean tech and sustainability, all vetted by our team.

With AI-driven impact projections, you’ll see how your investment reduces CO₂, saves energy, and conserves water—our way of supporting green entrepreneurship.
